About Me

Hello! I'm Samantha Katovich, a Data Science student at Purdue University Honors College graduating in May 2026. I'm passionate about using data and technology to solve real-world problems and create meaningful impact.

My experience spans product management, data analytics, and technical development. I've worked with Python, Java, JavaScript, SQL, and various data tools including PySpark, Hadoop, and Power BI.
