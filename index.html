About Me

Hello! I'm Samantha Katovich, a Data Science student at Purdue University Honors College graduating in May 2026. I'm passionate about using data and technology to solve real-world problems and create meaningful impact.

My experience spans product management, data analytics, and technical development. I've worked with Python, Java, JavaScript, SQL, and various data tools including PySpark, Hadoop, and Power BI. Currently, I'm a Product Management Intern at Consumers Credit Union, where I research market trends and develop data-driven product solutions. I also serve as Director of Admission Ambassadors at Purdue, managing recruitment and training for 100+ tour guides.

I'm particularly interested in machine learning, statistical modeling, and building applications that leverage big data. With certifications in Big Data for Social Good from Harvard Online and Google AI Essentials, I'm always looking to expand my technical skills and take on new challenges.
